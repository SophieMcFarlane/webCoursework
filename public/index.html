We are a welcoming, tight-knit club, with a work hard, but also play hard mentality. DUNC has 5 teams entered in BUCS leagues, and also runs a development squad to offer additional training to college netball players. BUCS games take place on Wednesday afternoons, and all members of the club enjoy attending and supporting the games of other teams. â€‹ Socials take place on Wednesday nights, and nothing is better than celebrating a whitewash of wins!
We are so fortunate to have 2 amazing coaches on board for the 2018/19 season, in Josie Pointon, and England International Leah Kennedy. They will split the coaching of all the teams between themselves according to their tactical specialities and team requirements.
1s and 2s have 2 weekly court training sessions 3s, 4s and 5s have 1 weekly court session All teams have both a strength and conditioning session and a fitness session every week. All members are expected to complete extra fitness sessions.
Last season saw us have our best season to date with the 1s promoted to Northern 1A, and a narrow 2nd in Conference Cup. Whilst the 2s promoted to Northern 2B and the 5s promoted to Northern 8B, and a narrow 2nd in Conference Plate.


Hall of Fame

Sophie McFarlane and Alex Taal succeeded in making the U21 Team Northmbria team for the 2017/18 season. They competed in the NPL U21 league against other Superleague teams. They also took part in the U21 summer tounament in Nottingham where Sophie lead the team as captain to two amazing victories over Severn Stars. Although it was a bitter sweet moment with Team Northumbria not existing anymnore, they thoroughly enjoyed their experience.
